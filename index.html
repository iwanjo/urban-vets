How UrbanVets Works

Step One...

We hire great vets.

Not just any doctor can become an UrbanVet. We thoroughly vet and hire only the best, highly qualified doctors. They become a part of our team, earn market-leading pay and benefits, and gain the freedom to choose when and where they work.

Next...

We partner with the best workplaces.

We've built partnerships with almost 500 forward-thinking, best-in-class hospitals and adoption shelters. We expect a lot from our vets, so the same applies to our hospitals. We partner with hospitals that not only have high medical standards, but also have a commitment to maintaining a healthy workplace for staff and patients.

The result?

Happy vets. Happy hospitals. Better medicine.

When doctors are in control of their schedules they are happier. Same goes for hospital teams who can breathe easier about their staffing. Together, our doctors and hospital partners are making the world of veterinary medicine a better, happier place.
